Beyond! Rock and Roll never dies!

Bio

Beyond was a Hong Kong rock band formed in 1983. The band is widely considered as the most successful and influential Cantopop band from Hong Kong. Wong Ka Kui, also known as the leader and main vocal of the band, died in 1993 during the filming of a show at Fuji Television in Tokyo. Beyond then official disbanded in 2005.
